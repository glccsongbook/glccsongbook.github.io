He Will Be Exalted

The King of Heaven

Left His rightful place on Heaven’s throne

He went to Calv’ry where He suffered all alone

The King was humbled when He bore the sins of every man

But one day our risen Lord is coming again



He will be exalted to the highest place

Every eye shall see Him and behold His face

To the mighty throne of Jesus, Jesus

All our worship we will bring

And proclaim Him Lord of lords and King of kings



The thunder crashes

To pronounce Christ Lord of all the earth

The lightning flashes to proclaim His matchless worth

Though men reject Him

Christ alone shall reign supreme at last

All His enemies shall wither like the grass
