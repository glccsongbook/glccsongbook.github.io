Whatever The Circumstance May Be

Saviour, Comforter, He’s always there for me

He brought me out of darkness

And caused the blind to see

Heavenly Father, take control of me

For whatever the circumstance may be

I will love Him and I know He loves me



So we’ll lift up our voice and sing unto our King

We are weak, He is strong

In His arms we belong

So we’ll lift up our hearts and rejoice unto our King

He is worthy, He is mighty

He can do anything for me



A sacrificial Lamb, He died for me

He laid His life on the cross

To rid us of our sin

When trials cloud our lives and nothing seems right

But whatever the circumstance may be

I will love Him and I know He loves me
