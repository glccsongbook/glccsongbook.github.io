Guidance

Though sands of time move on

And flowers grow and fade

As mountains rise and rivers flow

Your faithfulness remains

As generations flow

Your hand is seen in all

You guide our walk upon Your righteous paths

And catch us as we fall



We seek Your guidance, Lord

Your wisdom and faithfulness

The hand that will lead us on

The way that leads to home

We seek Your guidance, Lord

Your wisdom and faithfulness

Your eyes will watch over us

Each step of the way



You raise us when we’re weak

And guide us when we’re strong

As Father, Friend and Mentor

You will shelter us from harm

Though trials of life seem bleak

Your grace will lead us through

Your rod and staff will guide and shelter

Till in heaven home with You
