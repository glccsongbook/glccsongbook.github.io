Our Prayer

Dear Heavenly Father, we worship You

We want Your heart to rejoice in all we do

Your kingdom come, Your will be done

Dear Heavenly Father, we worship You



Thank You for giving us our daily bread

Teach us to forgive as You forgive

And lead us not into temptation

Lead us not into temptation

But deliver us, deliver us from evil



Wash us from sin and make us white as snow

Give us a clean heart to live for You

And cast us not out of Your presence

Cast us not out of Your presence

But restore to us the joy of Your salvation
